Discover our classes

You can attend to any of our classes!

Athletic class

Athletic classes take place in our Athletic Zones with sleds, battleropes and freestyle equipment for all over conditioning. Expect to drag, push, pull and swing your way to the finish line while developing functional strength, power and fitness. Suitable for all fitness levels and anyone who loves to test themselves.

Swimming class

Dive into a new activity like swimming! It’s a recreational activity enjoyed by many people either in open water, leisure clubs or public swimming pools. Open water swimming takes place in outdoor bodies of water such as the open sea, bays, lakes, rivers, canals and reservoirs. Make a splash !

Football classes

We create an atmosphere of fun, forming the best possible environment to learn, develop and make new friends. We’re confident we’re leading the way in how football should be delivered, no over-coaching, no over-complication, just simple, fun sessions.

Tennins class

Our Tennis Programs are available for Beginner, Improver, Intermediate and Advanced Level Players. If you want to learn to play through taking part in our tennis lessons, join a fun social matchplay group or compete at a high level we have something for you. Just look through the tennis lessons and courses available at any of our locations and you can book your place online with us today.

Bowling class

Individual lessons are perfect for the bowler looking for an intensive training sessions designed around their individual needs. Group lessons are designed to allow you to bring a group of your friends, teammates, or even the competition down to work out together with our coaches.

Basketball class

Ready to play basketball like the pros? Training sessions in London provide the opportunity to improve your skills and techniques regardless of whether you currently play in our leagues or not. By offering a 1.5 hour session of professional but friendly coaching and support, we give a service that compliments our fun and social leagues.

Karate class

KaraTe-Dō is one of the most widely practiced and popular Martial Arts in the world. According to the modern founder; Gichin Funakoshi Hanshi, Karate is not just a physical self defence practice but is also deeply rooted in the development of the individual through philosophy, spiritual humility and character. Due to its association with character building qualities, it has been successfully introduced into the physical education programmes across Europe. Today, Karate has become a global phenomenon and an extremely useful self development practice.
